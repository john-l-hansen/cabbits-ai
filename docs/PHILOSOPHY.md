## The Living World & Dynamic Generation

A core pillar of Cabbits is establishing a **living world through Agents as NPCs**. The world feels alive because NPCs are composed of a distinct **Skill** (e.g., a Blacksmith who physically crafts items within the engine) and an **Agent profile** (e.g., `BLACKSMITH.md`) that governs their behaviors, logic, and contributions to the world.

This enables **Dynamic World Generation**: NPCs live their lives and build things dynamically based on world events and user interactions. For example, if a user repeatedly inspects a steel dagger, the character's dialogue surrounding the dagger increases, which dynamically influences the NPCs to adapt and build accordingly.

## Purpose for Learning: Logic-First

The primary purpose of Cabbits is for learning, primarily focused on children (though accessible to anyone). 

The primary study and foundation of this experience is **Logic**. Logic always comes first. As the world expands and new areas are created, they must always be infused with a strong logic basis—extending deeply into disciplines like Mathematics and Language.

*These principles serve as the foundation of Cabbits and will infuse every aspect of this project as it continues.*
